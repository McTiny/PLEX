Background

The Practice

Independent consulting for AI-enabled design and smart automation.

Limited Energy eXperts (LEX) is an independent consulting practice focused on practical AI adoption for engineering, low-voltage design, and home automation workflows. LEX helps turn repetitive technical work into structured, repeatable processes — from Division 27 and Division 28 design support to AI-assisted estimating, documentation, coordination, and system planning.

The focus is not AI for its own sake. It is using AI where it actually helps: reducing manual effort, improving consistency, accelerating design decisions, and giving engineers, contractors, and homeowners better tools to solve real problems.

The free tools on this site — PLEX and The Estimator — came from real project needs. PLEX was built to simplify telecom room power, UPS, generator, and cooling coordination. The Estimator was built because low-voltage bidding still depends too heavily on spreadsheets that were never designed for Division 27/28 scope.

BICSI
Registered Communications Distribution Designer

The RCDD is BICSI's flagship credential for ICT systems designers. It validates expertise in the design and integration of communications systems, data centers, security systems, and the associated low-voltage infrastructure. Holders demonstrate competency across telecommunications distribution, structured cabling, and building systems coordination.
